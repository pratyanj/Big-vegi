### What is Big Vegi?
**Big Vegi** is a mobile-first collaborative web application designed to solve a common roommate dilemma: tracking shared vegetable and fruit purchases, dividing expenses accurately, and keeping an eye on price trends. 

Instead of manual spreadsheet entries, the app leverages **AI (Gemini 3 Flash)** to allow roommates to:
1. **Scan a Handwritten/Printed Receipt**: Instantly extracts items, prices, and quantities.
2. **Scan Raw Produce**: Take a photo of actual vegetables/fruits, and the AI automatically identifies what they are and estimates quantities.
3. **Log & Split Expenses**: Keeps a running log of purchases, showing who paid how much, and calculates balances (who owes whom) at the end of the month.
4. **Local Vocabulary integration**: Focuses heavily on fresh vegetables/fruits categorized with English names and their phonetic Gujarati equivalents (e.g., *Tomato (Tameta)*, *Lady Finger (Bhinda)*, *Ivy Gourd (Tindora)*, *Onion (Dungri)*).

## 2. Design System & Aesthetics

The visual language of **Big Vegi** is modern, organic, and clean. It avoids generic tech colors in favor of a natural, premium organic layout.

*   **Primary Color (60%):** **Emerald Green** (representing freshness, vegetables, nature, and growth).
*   **Secondary/Neutral Backgrounds:** **Warm Stone/Off-white** (cream-like, organic textures, eco-friendly feel, avoiding harsh pure whites/blacks).
*   **Accents:** 
    *   **Tomato Red / Carrot Orange** (energizing accents for highlights, delete actions, or chart lines).
    *   **Eggplant Purple / Blue** (cool secondary color for charts and trend tracking).
*   **Typography:** Modern, clean sans-serif (Inter/Outfit style).
*   **Vibe:** Friendly, lightweight, clean, organic, and premium.

## 3. Key Pages & Features
For context on where the logo and branding will be placed:
*   **Login Screen:** Minimalist screen with a clean Google Authentication button, showing the app name and main app icon.
*   **Dashboard:** Displays monthly total spend (₹ Rupees), recent trips, and a prominent floating action button to add new trips.
*   **New Trip (The Core AI Page):** 
    *   **"Scan Veggies or List"** button: Triggers the camera/file upload for the AI Scanner.
    *   **"Add Manually"** button: Triggers a list selector pre-populated with standard local vegetables.
*   **Financial Summary Page:** Contains beautiful Recharts diagrams:
    *   *Bar chart* showing spending breakdowns per flatmate.
    *   *Line chart* showing weekly/monthly price trends for top items (e.g., how the price of *Tameta* is fluctuating).

## 4. Logo & App Icon Requirements

We want to design a premium, recognizable app logo. It should represent the intersection of **fresh vegetables**, **sharing/collaboration**, and **modern smart technology**.

### Visual Metaphors to Combine:
1.  **Fresh Organic Produce:** Clean, minimalist shapes of vegetables (e.g., a green leaf, a tomato, a stylized onion/garlic, or a single fresh vegetable basket).
2.  **Shared Expenses / Collaboration:** Overlapping circular arcs, division grids, scales, or friendly minimal hands.
3.  **AI / Technology:** Subtle clean scan-lines, digital grid patterns, sparkle/magic icons, or geometric vector design.

### Logo Styles Preferred:
*   **Minimalist Vector:** Clean lines, solid shapes, high contrast, looks great as a small icon on a phone screen.
*   **Playful & Modern:** A cute, smiling, or stylized single giant veggie character (representing the "Big Vegi") wearing smart glasses or holding a digital scan element.
*   **Geometric Monogram:** A creative merger of the letter **"B"** and **"V"** shaped into a fresh leaf or a shopping bag.
